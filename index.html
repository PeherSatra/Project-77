Project on console

Visual Studio is an offline IDE. Go to the Visual Studio Code website and download Visual Studio. When entering Visual Studio, choose the theme you want. The main page will appear. Perform one of the shortcuts according to what you want and begin coding!

Right click on the screen and select Inspect. Then, click on "console" option on the top. The console will appear. To print anything on the console, add the "script" tag to the HTML and add the variables of what you want to add in the console. Then, put in the command, "console.log(name of your variable)" and the information on the variable will appear on the console.
